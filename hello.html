Coding on a Cell Phone

I am writing this HTML on my phone. I'm away from my computer and wanted to see if it was possible to do at least an emergency edit this way. So far I have discovered the following.

  • The Github Android app does not allow creating or editing.
  • The mobile website does.
  • It is really hard to do this on a phone.
  • This might be easier on my tablet where I at least have a keyboard.

And that is the result of this experiment. I could handle this in a pinch or for a really small job but it's not something I would want to do on a regular basis.
